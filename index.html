ClipMind requires a Groq API key to generate AI summaries. This key is:

3. YouTube Transcripts

When you request a summary, ClipMind fetches the video transcript from YouTube's public API. This transcript is sent to Groq's API to generate the summary. We do not store transcripts anywhere.

ClipMind stores your daily summary count (e.g., "3/10 free today") locally on your device only. This data never leaves your browser.

5. Third-Party Services

ClipMind uses Groq API (api.groq.com) to generate AI summaries. When you use ClipMind, your video transcript is sent to Groq for processing. Please review Groq's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.
